This project is a billing system of a company. Companies get rate sheets in  excel files of different international carriers.
The billing is automated(carr1, carr2, sample.csv, dat1.csv) files are used no manual entry is needed.
The rate sheets are sorted according to the city, country code(cc), area code(ac).
Also a Caller Detailed Record(CDR) Sheet of subscribers is also send to the company.
The ac's are sorted in ascending order.
Each record of the CDR has a called number which is compared with a combination of cc and ac and returns the rate of the maximum match.
The amount to be paid by each carrier with each country is printed.
